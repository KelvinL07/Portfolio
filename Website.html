Project Description

Over the past two years, I have gained hands-on experience in web and mobile development, creating dynamic websites and applications that effectively solve real-world problems. One of the key projects included the Food Image AI Recognition System, which involved designing and implementing an AI-driven solution capable of classifying food images into different categories. This project not only showcased my skills in machine learning and artificial intelligence, but also in web development, where the front end was developed using HTML and a Python-based framework. In addition, I worked on an IoT Smart Manufacturing project, where he integrated various IoT devices to improve production efficiency and data collection. This project leveraged my knowledge in IoT and data mining to enable smart and automated manufacturing processes. My commitment to learning and improving my skills was further reflected in his Smart Delivery Car IoT project, where he developed a smart delivery system that used IoT sensors and actuators to automate the delivery process. These experiences not only honed my technical skills, but also his ability to work in a team and effectively manage project timelines.
